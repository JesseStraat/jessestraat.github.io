<h4 class="research-title">Floer theory and quantum mechanics</h4>
<p class="research-desc">Floer thoery concerns the existence of periodic orbits of (classical) Hamiltonian systems. In particular, it can give a lower bound on the number of periodic orbits, known as cuplength estimates. I aim to expand this formalism to the quantum realm, using new methods and approximations. The ultimate goal is to improve our understanding of quantum mechanics with a dynamical approach.</p>
